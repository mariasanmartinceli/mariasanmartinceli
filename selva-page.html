The Peruvian jungle (selva) makes up for a vast majority of the country's land, and follows Brazil as the second largest region of the Amazon. This means it is vital for the counry's fauna and Earth's battle against CO2 emissions, but also vital for a number of Indigenous communities. The Peruvian Amazon holds some of the richest diversity in plant life, varying in size, colour, plant families medicinal properties and more. Click here to learn more.
