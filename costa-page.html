The Peruvian coast (costa) is the smallest geographical region of the three yet still holds approximately 53% of the Peruvian population! This region experiences mainly dry and hot weather with sandy terrain, however this varies throughout the region. Though the coast holds a variety of herbs and plant life endemic to Peru, many popular plants may also be native to other South American countries or regions across the globe.
