Caigua

Also known as Cyclanthera pedata L. Schard, Caigui.

The Caigua fruit sprouts from a collection of green vines, with a flat, oval shape. The fruit ranges from 10-15 centimeters long and comes in a variety of green shades. Past the outer layer, a white or light green flesh encases several black seeds. The raw taste can be compared to cucumbers, or green peppers when cooked.
