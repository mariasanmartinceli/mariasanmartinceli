Chusquea Delicatula

No recorded alternative names.

This bamboo plant can grow to about 3 meters tall and consists of long, thin stems which often drape to the ground. The leaves grow in increments down the stems, branching out in all directions. This species of delicatula can be found throughout the Andes mountains and near Machu Picchu, Peru.
